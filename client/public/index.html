Our Heritage & Legacy

Since 1990, Bastiramji Mithai Wale has been crafting the finest traditional Indian sweets with passion and dedication. Our journey began in a small kitchen with a big dream - to preserve and share the authentic flavors of Indian sweets. Today, we continue to uphold our family's legacy of excellence, using time-honored recipes passed down through generations.
