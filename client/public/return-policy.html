Return Policy

Last updated: March 2024

1. Return Eligibility

Due to the perishable nature of our products, we have a limited return policy:

  • Returns are only accepted for damaged or incorrect items
  • Returns must be initiated within 24 hours of delivery
  • Products must be in their original packaging
  • Products must not be consumed or opened

2. Return Process

To initiate a return:

  1. Contact our customer service within 24 hours of delivery
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. Take clear photos of the damaged/incorrect product
  4. Wait for our approval and return instructions

3. Refund Policy

Refunds will be processed as follows:

  • Full refund for damaged or incorrect items
  • Refund will be issued to the original payment method
  • Processing time: 5-7 business days
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable

4. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Consumed or opened products
  • Products past their expiry date
  • Products damaged due to improper storage
  • Products purchased during special promotions

5. Quality Issues

If you receive a product with quality issues:

  • Do not consume the product
  • Take clear photos of the issue
  • Contact us immediately
  • We will arrange for a replacement or refund

6. Shipping Returns

For approved returns:

  • We will provide a return shipping label
  • Package must be properly sealed
  • Return within 48 hours of approval
  • Keep the tracking number for reference
